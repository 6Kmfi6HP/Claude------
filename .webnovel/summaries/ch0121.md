## 剧情摘要
Chen Mo and Jiang Taichu sit across from each other at the stone platform under the dome. Jiang Taichu asks how many fragments Chen Mo collected; Chen Mo confirms all five. Chen Mo brings up the mysterious old man who has been searching for Jiang Taichu and found him too late. Jiang Taichu reveals he lost four emotions—fear, anger, joy, sorrow—and that the old man was right: it was already too late. Jiang Taichu asks Chen Mo how many emotions he has lost; Chen Mo says two: fear and anger. Jiang Taichu tells Chen Mo he still has time to decide whether to continue walking the path or stop. The lamp emits a faint fluctuation, reacting to the conversation. Chen Mo silently wonders what Jiang Taichu decided. Jiang Taichu says he decided to walk until he could go no further, but tells Chen Mo he does not need to walk the same path. Chen Mo does not give his answer. The lamp glows quietly, as if it also wants to know what this host will choose.
