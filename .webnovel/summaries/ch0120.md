## 剧情摘要
Chen Mo enters the dome space inside the barrier — a massive white-lit dome. At its center is a stone platform bearing ancient markings matching the three fragment sites. On it rests a fragment Chen Mo has never seen; Jiang Taichu reveals he separated it himself after all five fragments gathered in him, as a record of a secret he discovered. Chen Mo demands the truth about the Lamp. Jiang Taichu reveals: it is not a lamp — it is the world's spiritual vein, which sealed itself away before it could dry up. Chen Mo stands in long silence beneath the dome, then sits down to process the revelation.
